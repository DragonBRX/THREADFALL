**RECORTE SPRITES MULTI-POSE PERFEITO (ref imagem 1000029280.jpg 4 poses):**
- magick ref.jpg -crop 25%x100%+0%+0% pose1.png  # Exato bbox pose1 (manual preview display ref.jpg)
- Repeat pose2 (25%x+25%),3(+50%),4(+75%) – NO AUTO CROP (erra corpo/over).
- Clean Alpha "Pincel Erase": magick pose1.png -fuzz 15% -transparent black -morphology Erode Diamond:1 clean_pose1.png  # Remove extra pedaços/overlaps outros sprites.
- Batch 48 frames Knotfall: Gemini "Pixel art exato pose1 ref imagem, sombra capuz glow fios 128px PNG alpha" > imagemagick sheet.
